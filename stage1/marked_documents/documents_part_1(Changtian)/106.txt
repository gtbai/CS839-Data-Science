# For a Few Dollars More

{Manco} is a bounty killer chasing {El Indio} and his gang. During his hunting, he meets Col. {Douglas Mortimer}, another bounty killer, and they decide to make a partnership, chase the bad guys together and split the reward. During their enterprise, there will be lots of bullets and funny situations. In the end, one of the bounty hunters shows the real intention of his hunting.
