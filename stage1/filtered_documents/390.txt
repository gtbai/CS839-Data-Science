# Mystery Science Theater 3000

When zany mad scientist Dr. {Clayton Forrester} and his loopy assistant {Frank} get bored with their work at the Deep Thirteen research center, they kidnap {Joel} the janitor and shoot him into orbit on the Satellite of Love. While in space {Joel} builds wacky robot sidekicks {Tom Servo} and {Crow Robot} to help him withstand an onslaught of grade 'Z' movies that the mad scientists force him to watch.
